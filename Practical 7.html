Mahatma Gandhi

Father of the Nation

Leader of Indian Independence Movement

Biography

Mahatma Gandhi was a great leader of India's freedom struggle against British rule. He believed in the principles of truth and non-violence (Ahimsa) and inspired millions of people to join the movement for independence.

Major Movements

  • Non-Cooperation Movement (1920)
  • Civil Disobedience Movement (1930)
  • Dandi Salt March
  • Quit India Movement (1942)

Legacy

Mahatma Gandhi's philosophy of peace, truth, and non-violence influenced not only India but also many leaders across the world. His teachings continue to inspire people to follow the path of justice and harmony.
